Employment

Creative Director

X1 Ltd
1986 - 2019

Secondary education at Laytmer Upper School on a full scholarship.

On completing A levels found employment as an artworker before progressing to freelance design. Took time out to complete a theology degree. On graduating, purchased two of the first Apple Macintosh computers in the UK, along with a laser printer, and Pagemaker desktop publishing software.

Was offered studio space by a printing company in Slough. Within a year

I became a 33% share- holding director.

The past 33 years have seen me in that role, running the design studio, designing a wide range of printed products and helping X1 adapt to the massive technological changes of the past 3 decades, including introducing digital print and online services to the company.

Resigned as a Director in November 2018, and am currently working for X1 on a contract basis.
